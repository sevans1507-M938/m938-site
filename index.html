Our Mission

Sharing the Gospel.
Raising Leaders.
Transforming Lives.

At M938 Ministries, we are dedicated to advancing the Great Commission in Sierra Leone by reaching the lost with the Gospel, discipling believers, equipping leaders, and planting healthy, reproducing churches that bring lasting transformation to individuals and communities.

We focus on sustainable growth by training and empowering national leaders to build self-sustaining churches that impact their communities for Christ.
