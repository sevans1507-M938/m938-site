Why Your Gift Matters

Sierra Leone is one of the least-evangelized nations in West Africa. Your partnership directly funds:

  • Evangelistic outreach and Gospel campaigns
  • Discipleship groups and biblical training materials
  • Leadership development for national pastors
  • Church planting and local ministry support
  • Family living and ministry operational costs
